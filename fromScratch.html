My Tennis Story

I have been playing tennis for years and it has become one of my greatest passions. Competing in a league and earning 2nd place at the State Championship in 2024 has been one of my proudest achievements.

Beyond playing, I have spent the last 4 years coaching kids ages 6–11 out of my home in Alpine, Utah. Watching young players fall in love with the game is just as rewarding as competing myself.

Beginner Tips

Here are some of my top tips for getting started in tennis:

  1. Master the basics first
    • Learn a consistent forehand
    • Practice your backhand grip
    • Work on your serve every session
  2. Focus on footwork
    • Stay on your toes
    • Always recover to the center of the court
  3. Play smart, not just hard
    • Aim for consistency over power
    • Watch where your opponent is standing
    • Use cross-court shots to open up the court
  4. Have fun and stay patient
    • Tennis takes time to learn
    • Every player started as a beginner
